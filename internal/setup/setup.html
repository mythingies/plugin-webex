Setup Instructions
  1. Click "Create a New App" > "Create an Integration"
  2. Choose an app name
  3. Set Redirect URI to: wmcp://oauth-callback
  4. Select scopes:
    spark:messages_read spark:messages_write spark:rooms_read spark:memberships_read spark:people_read spark:kms meeting:schedules_read meeting:transcripts_read
  5. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret below

Clicking "Sign in with Webex" will open your browser for authentication.
Tokens auto-refresh — no manual renewal needed.

About Personal Access Tokens
  1. Go to the link above and log in to your Webex account
  2. Your token is displayed on the page (valid for 12 hours)
  3. Copy and paste it below
  4. PATs expire after 12 hours — use OAuth for persistent access
